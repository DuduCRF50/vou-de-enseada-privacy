Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

"Vou de Enseada" is an internal marketing analytics application operated by Enseada Hostel (CNPJ: 31.830.542/0001-15), located in Arraial do Cabo, RJ, Brazil. This application uses the Instagram Graph API to analyze the performance of our own Instagram Business Account (@enseadahostel) and publicly available data from competitor business accounts for benchmarking purposes.

2. Data We Collect

Through the Instagram Graph API, we access:

  • Our own account (@enseadahostel): Post metrics (impressions, reach, engagement), follower demographics, story insights, media metadata (captions, timestamps, like/comment counts).
  • Public competitor accounts: Publicly available data only — follower count, media count, biography, and public post engagement (likes, comments). We do NOT access private data, direct messages, or non-public information from competitor accounts.

3. How We Use the Data

All collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Monitoring the performance of our Instagram marketing efforts
  • Generating internal analytics reports and dashboards
  • Benchmarking against competitors to improve our content strategy
  • Guiding social media volunteers with data-driven content recommendations

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on a private, secured VPS (Ubuntu 24.04) with UFW firewall, Fail2ban, and SSH key-only access.
  • API tokens are stored in encrypted secrets management, never in plain text.
  • Access is restricted to the business owner (Carlos Eduardo Ribeiro Gomes) and authorized automated agents.
  • No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months for trend analysis. Users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

This application uses the Meta/Instagram Graph API. Use of this API is governed by Meta Platform Terms and Meta Developer Policies.

7. User Rights

In accordance with Brazilian LGPD (Lei Geral de Protecao de Dados), users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Revoke consent at any time

8. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact us at: contato@enseadahostel.com.br

You may also revoke this application's access to your Instagram data at any time through your Instagram Settings > Apps and Websites.
